Persona 1 — Primary

"Rahul the Researcher"

Serious fantasy player, data-hungry optimizer. Plays every match day during IPL and T20I series. Wants to beat the crowd through better data, not better luck.
Build for Rahul first · Free → Pro
Demographics
  • Age: 22–35, Tier 1/2 Indian city
  • Profession: Software engineer / MBA / finance
  • Income: ₹6–20 LPA
  • Cricket: Watches most IPL + T20I, understands strategy
  • Tech: Android primary, laptop secondary
Fantasy Behaviour
  • Platforms: Dream11 + MyTeam11
  • Frequency: Every IPL match + occasional T20Is
  • Contests: Grand leagues + H2H + small leagues
  • Spend: ₹50–500/match · ₹5K–30K/season
  • Research: 30–90 minutes per match
  • Teams: 3–8 variations per match
  • Result: Break-even territory · aspirational profitability

Jobs-to-be-Done

JOB 1 "Help me identify who is ACTUALLY in form" ▼
  • Not just IPL form — real current form across all T20s
  • Tell me if a player's form is rising or declining
  • Give me a number I can compare across players
  • Show the LAST 5–10 innings across ALL T20 leagues
JOB 2 "Help me find dangerous matchups" ▼
  • Which batters struggle against which bowlers?
  • Who dominates at this specific venue?
  • Who is weak against spin / pace / left-arm?
  • What are the key battles in tonight's match — as data, not pundit opinion
JOB 3 "Help me pick captain smartly" ▼
  • Captain = 2x points — most important single decision
  • Factor in form + venue + matchup + phase — not just "pick Kohli"
  • Give 2–3 options with risk levels and reasoning
  • Show confidence score for each pick
JOB 4 "Help me find differentials for grand leagues" ▼
  • In mega contests, same picks = average rank — I need edges
  • Low ownership + high ceiling = contest winners
  • Show estimated ownership vs expected output
  • Don't just show popular picks — show SMART picks
JOB 5 "Help me understand WHY, not just WHAT" ▼
  • I don't want a black box saying "pick Player X"
  • Show form score breakdown, matchup data, venue history
  • I want to learn and improve — not just follow tips
  • Transparency builds trust in the system
JOB 6 "Help me do all this in under 15 minutes" ▼
  • Currently takes 30–90 minutes across 6 open tabs
  • Want ONE source that synthesizes Cricbuzz + ESPN + YouTube + Telegram
  • More time ≠ better decisions if the data is right

Persona 2 — Primary · Largest User Group

"Priya the Pragmatist"

Regular fantasy player. Time-constrained. Just wants a decent team quickly — without reading spreadsheets or watching 15-minute YouTube videos.
Free Tier · Growth Asset · May Never Pay — That's OK
Profile
  • Age: 24–40 · Any Indian city · Also expat Indians in US/UK/UAE/AU
  • Profession: Working professional or homemaker who follows cricket
  • Tech: Mobile-only primarily
  • Cricket: Watches IPL casually · knows top 30–40 players
  • Fantasy: Dream11 only · ₹10–50 entry · 1–2 teams per match
Fantasy Behaviour
  • Research time: 5–15 minutes MAX
  • Season spend: ₹500–3,000
  • Motivation: Fun, social (friends play), occasional win
  • Result: Usually finishes bottom 50%
  • Info sources: WhatsApp group screenshots, Dream11 app, one YouTube video maybe

Jobs-to-be-Done

JOB 1"Just tell me who to pick"▼
  • I don't want to analyze data — I want THE answer
  • Give me a recommended team I can use directly
  • If I trust the system, I'll use it as-is
  • Make it as simple as opening WhatsApp
JOB 2"Tell me who to captain — one name, one reason"▼
  • I know captain matters (2x points)
  • I usually pick the most famous player by default
  • If someone reliable gives me a better option, I'll use it
JOB 3"Don't make me feel stupid"▼
  • "Strike rate in death overs vs left-arm pace" — means nothing to me
  • I want: 🟢 Strong pick · 🟡 Moderate · 🔴 Risky
  • Simple language: "Pick him — in great form at this ground"
  • No jargon, no analytics complexity
JOB 4"Help me stay competitive with my friends"▼
  • My friends play fantasy — I want to keep up without becoming an expert
  • If I consistently finish last, I'll stop playing
  • CricVeda should make me competitive, not expert
JOB 5"Be available when I need you — fast, mobile, 2 minutes"▼
  • I make my team 30–60 min before the match, often while commuting
  • Mobile-first is non-negotiable
  • Fast loading, minimal clicks
  • 2 minutes from opening app to having a team

Critical UX Requirements

Must Have
  • Mobile-first — works perfectly on phone
  • One-tap dream team view
  • Captain highlighted prominently
  • Confidence as 🟢🟡🔴, not "0.85"
  • Page loads in under 2 seconds
  • No account required for basic view
Why Priya Matters Even Free
  • Volume: Priyas are 60–70% of fantasy users
  • Word-of-mouth: tells friends, viral potential
  • Social proof: "10,000 users" matters for B2B sales
  • Funnel: some Priyas become Rahuls as they win more
  • Data: usage patterns improve algorithms

Sales Process

1
Discovery

PM sees CricVeda mentioned in Arjun's video. CTO finds API docs while researching cricket data. Or a developer on their team used CricVeda's free tier for a personal project.

2
Evaluation

CTO signs up, makes API calls, evaluates response quality vs CricAPI (data only) and building in-house (6 months, ₹30–50L). Internal pitch: "Smart Picks in 1 week for ₹4K/month."

3
Integration

Developer integrates 4–5 endpoints in 1 week. Builds "Smart Picks" tab. Tests with 5% of users (A/B). Result: 15–20% increase in pre-match engagement.

4
Full Rollout

Rolls out to all users. Upgrades to Business tier ($49/month). Becomes long-term customer. Provides testimonial for CricVeda marketing.

5
Expansion

Requests webhook support, white-label option, custom endpoints. Upgrades to Enterprise (custom pricing). Becomes reference customer for other platforms discovering CricVeda.
