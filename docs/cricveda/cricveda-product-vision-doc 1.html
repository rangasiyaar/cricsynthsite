Section 01

The Vision

"CricVeda is the world's most comprehensive, data-driven intelligence layer for T20 cricket — powering smarter fantasy decisions through deep ball-by-ball analytics across every T20 league, domestic tournament, and international match played globally."

Section 02

The Problem We Solve

Fantasy cricket is a $25B+ industry with 400M+ global players. Yet most team selections are built on fundamentally flawed foundations:

❌
Gut Feeling & Bias

Players pick based on loyalty and personal bias, ignoring data entirely.

"I always pick Kohli because he's Kohli"
❌
Hype & Social Narratives

Twitter trends and media narratives drive selections without analytical backing.

"Twitter says Player X is in form"
❌
Surface-Level Stats

Raw averages without context: no opponent quality, venue, phase, or matchup data.

"He averages 35 in T20s" (but against whom? at which venue?)
❌
Single-League Tunnel Vision

Missing 8 months of cross-league form because analysts only look at one competition.

"He had a bad IPL" (but dominated BBL, SA20, and T20Is since)
❌
No Cross-League Form

Debutants and overseas signings are evaluated blind with no comparable data.

"New overseas signing — should I pick him?" "No idea. No IPL data."

The result: Millions of fantasy teams built on incomplete, misleading, or entirely absent analysis.
The winners are those with BETTER DATA. CricVeda makes that data accessible to everyone.

Section 06

Our Data Philosophy

Principle 01
Global View, League Focus

We ingest ALL T20 data globally. We serve insights focused on the league the user cares about. A player's form is NEVER based on one league alone. Virat Kohli's form before IPL 2025 includes his T20I runs in January, not just IPL 2024 from May.

Principle 02
Honest Uncertainty

We NEVER pretend to know more than we do. Every score has a confidence level. Every data point has a source tag. If data is estimated, we say so. If data is missing, we say "limited data" not "no data available."

Principle 03
Open Foundations

Our raw data comes from open, legal sources. CricSheet (CC BY 4.0) for ball-by-ball. Wikidata (CC0) for player metadata. Our VALUE is in the analytics layer — the algorithms, scoring, and insights — not in hoarding data.

Principle 04
No Fragile Dependencies

We don't depend on scrapers that break. We don't depend on unofficial APIs that disappear. If a data source is unreliable, we build around it with manual processes, smart defaults, and transparent confidence scores. Boring but bulletproof beats clever but fragile.

Principle 05
Zero-Cost Sustainability

The product must be buildable, deployable, and maintainable at $0/month until it generates revenue. Free-tier services, open data, and pre-computation over real-time processing. We optimize for cost-efficiency, not vanity infrastructure.

Section 09

Core Beliefs

01
Data Without Context Is Noise

A batting average means nothing without knowing against whom, where, when, and in what form.

02
Cross-League Form Is the Unlock

No one else combines IPL + BBL + T20I + SA20 + PSL into a single form score. This is our edge.

03
Transparency Builds Trust

Users who see "confidence: 0.65" trust us MORE than platforms that show "form: 8.7" with no explanation. Honesty is a feature.

04
API-First Enables Scale

A dashboard serves hundreds. An API serves millions through partner platforms.

05
Free Data + Smart Algorithms = Moat

The data is open. Our moat is the intelligence layer — the algorithms, weighting, and insights that turn 4,500 matches of raw ball-by-ball into "pick Suryakumar as captain tonight."

06
Ship Boring, Iterate to Brilliant

Manual CSVs for metadata instead of fragile scrapers. Pre-computed insights instead of real-time queries. SQLite before PostgreSQL. Cron before streaming. Get it working, then make it elegant.
