Section 06

SAM — Serviceable Addressable Market

SAM = The portion of TAM we can actually reach with our specific product, distribution, and go-to-market approach.

Constraints That Narrow TAM → SAM

Constraint 1
Language: English-First

Excludes Hindi-only and regional language users (~30–40% of Indian market). Impact: Reduces Indian B2C addressable by ~40%.

~40% reduction India
Constraint 2
Tech Literacy: Data-Comfort Required

Full value requires comfort with data and analytics. Pure casual users won't read stats. Impact: Reduces B2C addressable by ~50%.

~50% reduction B2C
Constraint 3
Distribution: Organic-Only (No Ad Budget)

Twitter, Reddit, YouTube, Product Hunt. Cannot reach 200M users through organic alone. Realistic reach: 100K–1M users in Year 1–3.

Massive TAM reduction
Constraint 4
Geography: English-Speaking Markets

India (English-comfortable segment), Australia, UK, South Africa, expat communities globally. ~60–80M potential users vs 200M total.

Section 12

Market Growth Drivers

Driver 1
New T20 Leagues Launching

MLC (USA), SA20, ILT20, and more. Each new league = new fantasy contests = new users. CricVeda covers new leagues automatically — our parser is league-agnostic, CricSheet adds them.

Market grows without us doing anything special
Driver 2
Fantasy Cricket Expanding Beyond India

Australia (BBL fantasy growing, Dream11 entering), UK (The Hundred), South Africa (SA20), USA (MLC + large Indian diaspora), Pakistan (PSL growing rapidly). CricVeda's cross-league coverage is perfectly positioned.

International expansion ready
Driver 3
Regulation Legitimizing Fantasy Sports

India: DPIIT recognizes fantasy sports as skill-based (not gambling). More regulation = more corporate sponsors = more users = bigger market. States gradually lifting bans. Legitimate market grows faster than grey market.

Regulatory tailwind
Driver 4
AI/ML Making Analytics More Valuable

Users increasingly expect "smart" features. Fantasy platforms need to differentiate — "AI-powered picks" is a marketing advantage. CricVeda provides the intelligence layer platforms can label as "AI-powered."

Rising expectations → rising WTP
Driver 5
Cricket's Global Commercial Growth

Cricket in 2028 Olympics (LA) → massive global awareness spike. ICC expanding T20 World Cup (20 teams). Women's cricket growing. Cricket investment growing ($5B+ in IPL alone). Bigger sport = bigger fantasy market = bigger TAM.

Long-term structural tailwind
Driver 6
Creator Economy Growth

More cricket content creators every year. Fantasy cricket niche especially fast-growing. Creators need differentiated data sources. Creator adoption is self-reinforcing: more creators → more visibility → more users → more creators want in.

Self-reinforcing flywheel
