Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to CricSynthesis. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users and enterprise partners. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you access our API products (CricVeda, MatchSynth, and GraphSynth) and our website.

2. Information We Collect

As a B2B API provider, we primarily collect information related to your organization's usage of our services. The types of data we process include:

  • Account Information: Names, business email addresses, organization details, and API credentials required to grant access to the private beta.
  • Usage Data & Telemetry: API request volumes, endpoints accessed, latency, IP addresses, and generalized metadata to monitor system health and rate limits.
  • User-Input Data: Data passed directly into the API (such as player IDs, context configurations, or custom match conditions) necessary to generate computational results, predictions, or simulations.

3. How We Use Your Information

We process your data exclusively for operational and product improvement purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and secure the CricSynthesis API endpoints.
  • To enforce our terms of service, including rate limiting (e.g., quotas for CricVeda API calls).
  • To understand usage patterns to optimize machine learning models underlying MatchSynth and CricVeda.
  • To provide technical support and alert you to system statuses.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your organization's data or telemetry logs to third parties. We may disclose your information only in the following scenarios:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (e.g., AWS, GCP) and infrastructure partners bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law to comply with judicial proceedings or protect the rights, property, or safety of CricSynthesis or others.

5. Data Security

We employ enterprise-grade security measures to protect your data. All data in transit to our API endpoints is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher. Access to API keys is strictly monitored, and our underlying infrastructure operates on secure cloud environments. However, no transmission method over the internet is completely immune from intrusion.

6. API Anonymity and Specifics

Our predictive models are trained on historical, publicly available cricket data (over 15,000 matches) and simulated environments. We explicitly do not log or store personally identifiable information (PII) of your end-users. All requests sent to CricSynthesis APIs must be properly anonymized regarding your end-users before transmission.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our products evolve out of private beta. We will notify API developers via email and update the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@cricsynthesis.in.
